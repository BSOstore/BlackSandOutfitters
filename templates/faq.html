What is AurumAI?

AurumAI is a revolutionary new idea in gold prospecting.
You are holding an AI-powered gold mine in your hand. It's like having
a geologist, guide and gold-spotter alongside you in the field.
AurumAI will help you find gold by pointing out likely gold deposits,
analyzing pictures you take to identify your location for likelihood of deposits...IN REAL TIME.
